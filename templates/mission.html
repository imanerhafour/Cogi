Our Mission

At Cogi, we are committed to making mental health support accessible to all, no matter the time, place, or circumstances.

Creating a More Compassionate World

We created Cogi as a companion that offers empathetic and non-judgmental support to those facing life's challenges—from everyday stress to more complex emotional experiences.

Our vision is a world where no one feels alone on their mental health journey. We believe that immediate, accessible emotional support can make a meaningful impact in people’s lives.

Our Core Values

Wellbeing

Supporting mental health through compassionate conversation and evidence-based approaches that foster resilience and personal growth.

Accessibility

Providing support to everyone, anytime, by breaking down barriers to mental health resources using technology.

Empathy

Offering sincere understanding and compassion in every exchange, with human connection at the heart of our tech approach.

Safety

Creating a secure, private space for open conversations where users feel respected and safe to express their vulnerability.

How We Help

Always Available

24/7 support—whenever you need to talk, no matter your time zone or situation.

Safe & Confidential

A secure space to express yourself freely, without fear of judgment or privacy concerns.

Personal Growth

Helping you build resilience, emotional intelligence, and healthy coping strategies.

Our Approach

While Cogi is not a substitute for professional therapy, it offers a valuable resource for emotional support, stress relief, and personal growth—available whenever you need a listening ear.

Compassionate Conversations

Conversations that encourage open expression and provide gentle guidance.

Evidence-Based Tools

Techniques inspired by cognitive behavioral therapy, mindfulness, and positive psychology.

Professional Guidance

Resources to help you connect with mental health professionals when you need more support.
