Frequently Asked Questions

Is Cogi a replacement for therapy?

No, Cogi is designed to be a supportive companion but does not replace professional mental health care. We encourage users to seek professional help when needed.

Is my conversation with Cogi private?

Yes, your privacy is our priority. All conversations are encrypted and confidential. Read our privacy policy to learn more about how we protect your data.

How much does Cogi cost?

We offer both free and premium plans. The basic features are always free, while our premium plan offers additional resources and features for a monthly subscription.

Can I use Cogi on my phone?

Yes, Cogi is available on web, iOS, and Android platforms, so you can access support wherever you are.
